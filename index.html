Oasis

Co-founded Oasis, an eight-week educational program for students with no prior experience who want to build their first website or app. The program pairs participants with mentors and teammates, providing structured learning through weekly "Hack Sessions." Originally founded in Fall 2020 as a Sandbox program with virtual workshops and mentor networks, Oasis evolved and became an independent club in Spring 2023. The organization is built on three core principles: remaining accessible to all learners, emphasizing metacognitive skills over specific frameworks, and believing that the best way to learn is to learn by doing.
